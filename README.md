# Nexus Intelligence

**Autonomous market intelligence agent** providing regime detection, signal-to-noise analysis, technical levels, daily market synthesis, and multi-model research — available via MCP protocol.

Built by a 25-year fire service veteran who builds AI systems and invests in AI infrastructure and electrification.

## What Nexus Does

Nexus monitors 57 instruments across 7 composites daily. It ingests hundreds of sources, scores them through a proprietary trust framework (RAIM), and separates what price is doing from what everyone is saying about it.

When credit diverges from equities — it flags it.  
When VIX compression masks structural damage — it names it.  
When the consensus reads a signal wrong — it explains why.

## Research Engine

The research engine uses multi-model adversarial synthesis:
- **Quick** (~2 min) — Existing knowledge + single cloud model
- **Standard** (~10 min) — Web search + 3-pass Claude Sonnet synthesis
- **Deep** (~25 min) — Council of Experts (Claude + Grok + GPT) with rebuttals, RAIM trust validation, and CIO synthesis

Every research output includes: thesis, confidence score, invalidation criteria, time horizon, source trust ratings, contradiction analysis, and regime context.

## Signal to Noise Indicators

Six proprietary indicators calibrated daily:
1. **Nexus Consensus Index (NCI)** — What the crowd thinks vs what price says
2. **Stress Index** — Composite market stress reading
3. **Price-Narrative Divergence** — Gap between price action and narrative
4. **Narrative Quality Index** — How good is the reasoning behind consensus
5. **Capitulation Score** — Proximity to capitulation conditions
6. **Thrust Score** — Momentum breadth and conviction
